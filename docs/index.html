Who we are

The Blockchain Institute is a 501(c)(3) public charity that provides blockchain education for everyone that wants to learn. We promote the adoption, development, and use of blockchain technology.

We think education is the best way forward and invite you to join us as we build the web of the future.

In-Person
Classes

You don't have to learn alone. If you are in Chicago, sign up for one of our free in-person training sessions. Our Crypto Curious Course is for anyone interested in learning more about Bitcoin and Blockchain Technology.

Corporate
Training

Your employees need to be aware of how Blockchain and Decentralized Technologies are going to change the landscape of how we do business. Blockchain is no magic bullet, but what it does do, nothing else can. Contact us about specialized training for your team.

Events

Do you want to get involved in Chicago's growing Crypto and Blockchain community? We've made it easy for you by collecting the best events taking place in our space and around the city. Meetups, Speakers, Networking, Socials, Hackathons, Classes, Workshops - we have it all!

Online Courses

If you want to start learning now, our online courses are for you! Our lessons are broken down into easy to understand parts so you can go through the material at your own page.
